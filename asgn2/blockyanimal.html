A few notes:

For my blocky animal I created the character from the Roblox mini game Dress to Impress. The animation is this character performing 'pose 28' which is an extremely popular pose turned meme, especially recognizable for a younger audience. The character leans back with their arms behind their back and a large stance with movement in her back, connecting sholders, arms, hips, quads, calves, (feet for original) as well as head and neck for the 2 and 3 moving connection requirements. This reqirement can be demonstrated with three joint movement on the first or second animation using the slider. For my shift click animation I made the Duolingo owl on theme with the game, as if it was a dress up theme and it performs a flying animation with multiple spherical eyes and a beak which moves to speak.
